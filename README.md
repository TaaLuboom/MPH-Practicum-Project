An evaluation report based on pre- and post-DSMES participant surveys to measure program impact. It was developed by comparing baseline data from 49 participants with post-intervention results from 33 participants to track changes in diabetes knowledge and distress levels. The analysis utilized statistical testing to confirm a significant increase in participant knowledge, even while identifying persistent emotional challenges like burnout. These findings will be used to guide future iterations of the program, specifically recommending more targeted psychosocial support and culturally tailored nutritional interventions. Ultimately, it helps justify the program's expansion while identifying minor logistical improvements to enhance the participant experience.
